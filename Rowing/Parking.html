Parking Reservations

At many regattas parking is limited,
and the best way to ensure you can get a spot to park your car is to view the parking prices below before you vist. The tickets can cost anywhere from $3 to $30 with discounts for the elderly and for adults with children. View the prices below to buy your ticket form the next scheduled regatta!

Senior Citizens Pricing

  • With Season Pass

    • Early Morning Hours Before 11 A.M. $3
    • Afternoon $5

  • Without Season Pass

    • Early Morning Hours Before 11 A.M. $7
    • Afternoon $9

Adults with Children

  • With Season Pass

    • Early Morning Hours Before 11 A.M. $7.50
    • Afternoon $10

  • Without Season Pass

    • Early Morning Hours Before 11 A.M. $15
    • Afternoon $20

Adults without Children

  • With Season Pass

    • Early Morning Hours Before 11 A.M. $12.50
    • Afternoon $15

  • Without Season Pass

    • Early Morning Hours Before 11 A.M. $25
    • Afternoon $30
